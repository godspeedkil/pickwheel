✦PickWheel
Winner —

Tap SPIN or press Space to go — tap again to cancel mid-spin. The wheel picks a weighted-random winner the instant you spin; the animation just shows you the result.

Items

Give each item a weight — bigger weight, bigger slice. Click a dot to recolor it, or switch palettes in Settings to recolor everything at once.

Batch add

One item per line: Name, weight. Weight is optional and defaults to 1.

Wheel title

The editable name at the top of the page (e.g. "Untitled Wheel").

Spin behavior

Randomize starting anglePick a new resting angle before every spin
Idle spinWheel rotates slowly for looks when it's not spinning — never produces a result

Winner banner image

Add images to individual items in the Items tab. When an item with an image wins, these control how it's shown.

Wheel colors

Switching palettes recolors every item to match — any custom colors you've picked are reset.

Build your own

Add colors, name it, and save it — it'll show up under My Palettes above.

Sound design

Pick from a library of generated sounds, or upload your own clips (MP3, WAV, OGG, and more — up to 25 clips, ~3.5MB each) and one plays at random each spin.

Saved wheels

Save your items, colors and settings under a name, and reload them anytime.
